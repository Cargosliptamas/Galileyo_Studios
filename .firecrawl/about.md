About

# Galileyo Studios

Original films from the front lines of the new resistance.

We make cinematic short-form content funded by the audience, produced independently, and distributed without asking permission. No studio executives. No algorithm. No gate. Just the work.

The Mission

## Built for the people the rest of media will not touch.

Galileyo Studios exists because the stories that matter most are the ones nobody else will fund. The themes too real. The characters too human. The stakes too close to home.

Seekin Destroys is our first series. Seven episodes that trace the arc of a world that lost its ability to communicate, and the people who build a new way to fight back.

Every episode is funded directly by the people who want to see it made. No advertisers. No executives. The audience is the studio.
